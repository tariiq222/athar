# Design Context — أثر (Athar)

> Source of truth for all visual and UX decisions. Updated 2026-06-30 via `impeccable teach`.
> Arabic-first Saudi SaaS for SMB social-media content (LinkedIn / X).

---

## Users

### Primary persona — فهد (Fahd)
- **Context**: SMB owner (e-commerce store, restaurant, café, local service), 28–45, runs everything solo or with a tiny team. Time-poor. Mobile-first (checks between tasks).
- **Job to be done**: Maintain a regular, professional presence that brings customers — with minimum time investment.
- **Emotional goal**: confidence that his brand looks "real and professional" without taking over his day. Fears looking amateurish or "AI-generated".
- **Decision trigger**: visible sample content for *his* brand, clear price, low friction. Trial matters.

### Secondary persona — نورة (Noura)
- **Context**: Founder / executive / consultant (tech, business, real estate, legal), 30–50, building personal brand on **LinkedIn** (+ sometimes X) for reputation and inbound opportunities.
- **Job to be done**: Publish expert opinion / experience regularly without spending hours writing.
- **Emotional goal**: credibility and authenticity — content must read as "written by her, not by AI". Hates "AI-translated" tone.
- **Decision trigger**: sample that sounds like her voice + visible sourced facts.

### Common ground
Both dread: generic ChatGPT tone, English-tool Arabic translation, agencies that take 3+ SAR and weeks. Both reward: Arabic-native quality, sourced facts, brand voice match, image quality.

## Brand Identity

### Name & promise
- **Name**: أثر (Athar) — *trace, effect, impact*
- **Tagline**: من المعرفة إلى الأثر — "From knowledge to impact"
- **Brand values** (in order of visual weight):
  - **أثر** (Impact) — primary
  - **إبداع** (Creativity)
  - **معرفة** (Knowledge)
  - **ثقة** (Trust)

### Personality (3 words)
**حاد · ماهر · مباشر** — *Sharp · Expert · Direct*

The product exists because Arabic content quality is broken. The UI must embody that promise — never feel translated, never feel decorative-for-its-own-sake. Confidence without ceremony.

### Logo
Sophisticated geometric monogram: dark-green + cream interlock with a circular dot accent (upper position). Two interlocking leaf-like forms suggest "from → to" (knowledge → impact). The mark reads at any size and is the strongest visual identifier.

## Aesthetic Direction

### Direction
**Refined / Luxury** — premium tool feel, suitable for a 599 SAR/month product aimed at executives and SMB owners who judge by polish. **Not** maximalist, **not** ornamented.

### Reference (commit to this)
**Linear · Arc · Vercel** — premium SaaS with obsessive detail, restrained color use, sophisticated motion, editorial typography. Engrave the rhythm; copy the restraint.

### Anti-reference (explicit)
Generic admin dashboards, "every SaaS" sameness. No Buffer/Hootsuite/Canva vibes. No "AI slop" tells (gradients on text, glassmorphism everywhere, side-stripe borders, generic card grids, hero-metric template, monospace-as-tech).

### Theme
**Light only.** SMB owners review content during morning coffee and between tasks, in lit offices and cafés. Light surface reads as "professional document" — dark would read as "developer tool". The brand palette is calibrated for light surfaces.

### Color rules (binding)
- **60-30-10 weight rule** (by visual weight, not pixel count): 60% Primary deep + cream, 30% Primary green + neutral border, 10% terracotta + mint.
- **Cream is the page surface.** White is reserved for elevated surfaces (modals, popovers). Never invert.
- **No raw grays** — every neutral is tinted toward the green hue family (`oklch(L 0.005-0.02 155)`).
- **Terracotta is rare.** Reserved for "featured" moments — billing CTA, plan upgrade, single attention-pulling element per view. Overuse kills it.
- **No gradient text. No side-stripe borders. No glassmorphism.** Ever.
- **Primary green on white** needs ≥ 4.5:1 contrast for text; pair with white text on primary deep for buttons.
- **Status colors** (success / warning / error): built from the same hue family — green-tinted, terracotta-tinted, navy-tinted. Avoid pure red/yellow.

## Typography

### Pairing

| Role | Arabic | Latin | Weights | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Display** (H1, hero, brand) | **TheYearofHandicrafts** | **Effra Heavy / Black** | Reg, Med, SemiBold, Bold, Black | Local: `/Users/tariq/Desktop/خط عام الحرف اليدوية 2025/` (TTF/OTF) |
| **Body** (UI, paragraphs, tables) | **IBM Plex Sans Arabic** | **Effra Regular / Medium** | Reg, Med, SemiBold, Bold | Next/font (Google Fonts CDN) |
| **Numeric / data** | **IBM Plex Sans Arabic** | **Effra Std Lt / Rg** | tabular figures | — |

The display face (TheYearofHandicrafts) carries the brand voice at heading scale. Body stays calm in IBM Plex Sans Arabic so the content reads cleanly. Effra handles all Latin (URLs, English brand terms, system strings).

### Scale (5 steps, ≥1.25 ratio, fixed rem for product UI)

| Token | Size | Line-height | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| `--text-display` | 2.5rem (40px) | 1.15 | Page hero H1 only |
| `--text-h2` | 2rem (32px) | 1.2 | Section headings |
| `--text-h3` | 1.5rem (24px) | 1.3 | Card / panel headings |
| `--text-body-lg` | 1.125rem (18px) | 1.6 | Featured body, lead paragraphs |
| `--text-body` | 1rem (16px) | 1.6 | Default UI body, paragraphs |
| `--text-sm` | 0.875rem (14px) | 1.5 | Metadata, helper text, table cells |
| `--text-xs` | 0.75rem (12px) | 1.4 | Labels (uppercase tracking), captions |

### Typography rules (binding)
- **Body line-length cap: 65–75ch.** Anything wider fatigues the eye.
- **Headings: Display face, primary deep or navy color, tight tracking** (`-0.01em` on display).
- **Body: IBM Plex Sans Arabic Regular (400)**, never lighter; bold only for emphasis.
- **Numbers**: tabular figures everywhere (`font-feature-settings: "tnum"`).
- **Line-height on dark backgrounds**: add +0.05 to body line-height (light text reads lighter).
- **RTL-aware**: typographic decisions stay the same; only the *direction* flips.
- **No all-caps for long passages** — reserved for short labels (form labels, table headers) with `letter-spacing: 0.05em`.

## Layout & Space

### Scale (4pt base)
`4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96` — semantic tokens: `--space-xs` (4), `--space-sm` (8), `--space-md` (12), `--space-base` (16), `--space-lg` (24), `--space-xl` (32), `--space-2xl` (48), `--space-3xl` (64), `--space-4xl` (96).

### Layout rules (binding)
- **`gap` over margins** — no margin collapse, no cleanup hacks.
- **Mobile-first, fluid**: build from 360px up; use `clamp()` for hero/display sizes only (not body).
- **Container queries** for components (cards adapt to sidebar width); viewport queries for page layout.
- **Card grid** uses `grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(280px, 1fr))` — breakpoint-free.
- **Cream is the canvas**, not a card. Not everything needs a card container; flat hierarchy reads cleaner.
- **No nested cards.** If a card needs sections, use dividers and padding, not nested boxes.
- **Left-aligned (start-aligned in RTL) text** with asymmetric compositions — refined luxury feels *designed*, not symmetrical.
- **Touch targets ≥ 44×44px** on mobile.

## Motion

### Principles
- **One orchestrated moment beats scattered micro-interactions.** Page-load with staggered reveals on the editor / dashboard hero only.
- **Exponential easing** (`ease-out-quart` / `ease-out-expo`) for natural deceleration.
- **No bounce, no elastic.** Real objects decelerate smoothly.
- **Transform + opacity only.** Never animate `width`/`height`/`padding`/`margin`. Use `grid-template-rows: 0fr → 1fr` for height reveals.
- **Reduced motion honored** (`prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` → durations ≤ 50ms, no transforms).

### Timing tokens
- `--motion-instant` 80ms · `--motion-quick` 160ms · `--motion-base` 240ms · `--motion-slow` 400ms · `--motion-orchestrated` 600ms

---

## Iconography

- **Single set**: stroked geometric icons, 1.5px stroke, rounded caps, 24×24 viewport.
- **Directional icons** (back/forward, arrows, send) **mirror in RTL** via `transform: scaleX(-1)` or per-direction icon swaps.
- **Non-directional icons** (search, settings, calendar) **do not mirror**.
- **No icons above every heading.** A heading with an icon above it is the AI-template tell.

## Anti-Patterns (the "AI Slop Test")

Reject any of these on sight:

| Pattern | Why banned |
|---|---|
| Side-stripe borders (`border-left: 3px solid ...`) on cards / callouts | Most overused admin/medical UI tell |
| Gradient text (`background-clip: text` + gradient fill) | Decorative without meaning |
| Glassmorphism everywhere (`backdrop-blur` on cards) | Lazy "premium" cosplay |
| Identical card grids (icon + heading + text, repeated) | Reads as templated |
| Hero metric template (big number + small label + gradient accent) | SaaS dashboard cliché |
| Large icon with rounded square above every heading | Generic AI-template |
| Monospace as "technical" shorthand | Lazy typography |
| Pure black `#000` or pure white `#fff` for surfaces | Never appears in nature |
| Centered everything | Refined luxury is asymmetric |

## Accessibility

### Level
**WCAG 2.1 AA** as the V1 mandatory baseline + **AAA selectively** for content-critical surfaces (approved post body, billing amounts, status badges, error messages). Full AAA remains a stretch goal in this file — V1 ships AA.

### Binding rules (AA minimum)
- **Contrast**: AA = 4.5:1 normal text, 3:1 large text (≥18pt). Primary deep on cream: ~12:1 ✅. Primary green on cream: ~5.5:1 ✅ (large + body via weight ≥600). Terracotta on cream: verify before use.
- **AAA where it matters**: body of approved posts (the content the user ships), billing amounts and dates, every `StatusBadge` label, every error state. Verify 7:1 for these specifically.
- **Color is never the only signal.** Status badges always pair color + text + accessible icon.
- **Keyboard**: full keyboard navigation with visible focus rings (2px primary green outline + 2px offset). RTL focus order: right → left, top → bottom.
- **Screen reader**: semantic HTML, `aria-live` regions for async content (month-plan progress), accessible Arabic names everywhere.
- **Form errors**: `aria-describedby` wiring on every input; error text in Arabic with RTL layout.
- **Reduced motion**: mandatory support; long orchestration animations collapse to instant.
- **Touch targets**: ≥ 44×44px on mobile.
- **RTL focus mirroring**: tab order reverses naturally with `dir="rtl"`.

## Design Principles (the 5 rules)

These are the questions to ask before adding any element:

1. **النص أولاً (Text-first).** Typography is the brand. Content is the asset; chrome stays out of the way. If a feature is mostly UI around text, the chrome is wrong.
2. **صريح بلا فجاجة (Direct without rudeness).** No decorative elements. Every element earns its place. Premium tools trust the user.
3. **تفصيل محسوب (Calculated detail).** One orchestrated moment beats scattered micro-interactions. Subtle motion only where it matters.
4. **عربية أصيلة (Arabic-native).** Built for Arabic typography and RTL from day one — never "translated to Arabic".
5. **ثقة في الفراغ (Trust in negative space).** Refined luxury lives in what's missing, not what's added.

## What "good" looks like for أثر

A user opens the post editor. The page is cream. A single bold display headline in TheYearofHandicrafts at the top (primary deep). The post text below in IBM Plex Sans Arabic — calm, generous line-height, no chrome competing. To the right (logical "end" in RTL): a quiet CharCounter in primary green. Sources sit below the text in a flat list — no card, just text-with-link in muted brown. The image preview is a single large rectangle with subtle hover state. The "approve" button is primary green, sharp rectangle with no rounding excess. The page-load animation is one orchestrated reveal: heading settles, text fades up 60ms later, sidebar 120ms after — done in 600ms total. Then it is still.

That's premium. That's أثر.

    ## ٣.٦ حوكمة المكوّنات والأيقونات

### ٣.٦.١ نظام الأيقونات

- **المكتبة الوحيدة المعتمدة:** `lucide-react`.
- ممنوع خلط مكتبات أيقونات متعددة مثل Heroicons أو Material Icons أو Font Awesome أو SVGs عشوائية داخل الواجهة.
- الاستثناء الوحيد: شعار أثر وأيقونات العلامة التجارية الرسمية داخل `public/brand/`.
- النمط المعتمد: stroked geometric icons فقط.
- `strokeWidth` الافتراضي: `1.5`.
- الأحجام المسموحة:
  - `16px`: داخل النصوص، الحقول، metadata.
  - `20px`: الأزرار والإجراءات.
  - `24px`: التنقل، عناوين الأقسام، الحالات الفارغة.
- لا تستخدم أيقونة منفردة كإجراء بدون `aria-label` عربي واضح.
- Tooltip إلزامي للأزرار التي تحتوي أيقونة فقط على الشاشات الكبيرة.
- الأيقونات الاتجاهية فقط تُعكس في RTL، مثل:
  - سهم التالي/السابق.
  - إرسال.
  - فتح رابط خارجي عند الحاجة.
- الأيقونات غير الاتجاهية لا تُعكس، مثل:
  - بحث.
  - تقويم.
  - إعدادات.
  - حذف.
  - نسخ.
- لا توضع أيقونة فوق كل عنوان أو بطاقة بشكل تلقائي. الأيقونة تستخدم فقط عندما تضيف معنى وظيفياً واضحاً.

### ٣.٦.٢ طبقات المكوّنات

تنقسم المكوّنات إلى ثلاث طبقات فقط:

```text
components/
  ui/          # عناصر واجهة عامة ومحايدة المجال
  shared/      # مكونات مرتبطة بمجال أثر وتستخدم في أكثر من شاشة
  layout/      # هيكل التطبيق والتنقل
```

#### `components/ui/`

مكونات عامة لا تعرف شيئاً عن البوستات أو العلامة التجارية أو الفوترة:

```text
components/ui/
  Button/
  IconButton/
  Input/
  Textarea/
  Select/
  Checkbox/
  RadioGroup/
  Switch/
  FormField/
  Modal/
  Dialog/
  DropdownMenu/
  Popover/
  Tooltip/
  Tabs/
  Pagination/
  Spinner/
  Divider/
```

القواعد:

- لا يجوز لأي صفحة بناء Button أو Input أو Modal أو Tooltip محلي خاص بها.
- كل مكوّن `ui` يستخدم tokens فقط للألوان والمسافات والخطوط.
- لا يقبل المكوّن ألواناً raw أو `className` يغير هويته الأساسية.
- المكونات تدعم `loading` و`disabled` و`error` عند الحاجة.
- جميع الحقول تدعم:
  - `label`
  - `hint`
  - `error`
  - `aria-describedby`
  - حالة disabled
  - RTL بشكل افتراضي

#### `components/shared/`

مكونات مرتبطة بمنتج أثر وتستخدم في أكثر من شاشة:

```text
components/shared/
  StatusBadge/
  PostCard/
  SourceList/
  ImagePreview/
  CharCounter/
  EmptyState/
  ErrorState/
  Skeleton/
  Toast/
  PlatformBadge/
  PageHeader/
  FilterBar/
```

القواعد:

- أي عنصر يظهر في أكثر من صفحة أو أكثر من تدفق واحد يجب أن ينتقل إلى `shared/`.
- `StatusBadge` هو المسار الوحيد لعرض حالة البوست.
- `PostCard` هو المسار الوحيد لعرض ملخص البوست في القوائم واللوحة.
- لا تنشئ الصفحة نسخة مختلفة من `EmptyState` أو `ErrorState` أو `Skeleton`.
- اختلافات العرض تكون عبر `variant` أو `size` أو props محددة، لا عبر نسخ مكون جديد.

#### `components/layout/`

```text
components/layout/
  AppShell/
  Header/
  BottomNav/
  SideNav/
  PageContainer/
  ContentSection/
```

القواعد:

- لا تبني أي صفحة navigation أو header أو page container خاص بها.
- كل صفحات `(app)` تستخدم `AppShell`.
- صفحات `(auth)` تستخدم `AuthLayout` منفصل.
- التنقل السفلي للموبايل والتنقل الجانبي للديسكتوب يستهلكان نفس تعريف الوجهات من ملف واحد.

### ٣.٦.٣ قاعدة عدم التكرار

- أي مكوّن يستخدم في مكانين وينطبق عليه الشكل والسلوك نفسه تقريباً يصبح Shared Component.
- أي style block يتكرر في أكثر من ملفين يجب تحويله إلى token أو مكوّن.
- أي منطق عرض متكرر يجب أن ينتقل إلى helper أو component مشترك.
- ممنوع نسخ JSX بين الصفحات مع تعديل بسيط في النص أو اللون.
- قبل إنشاء مكوّن جديد، يجب البحث أولاً داخل:
  - `components/ui/`
  - `components/shared/`
  - `components/layout/`

### ٣.٦.٤ قاعدة ملكية المكوّن

| الحالة | المكان الصحيح |
|---|---|
| زر أو حقل أو نافذة أو Tooltip | `components/ui/` |
| عنصر خاص بأثر ويتكرر بين الشاشات | `components/shared/` |
| عنصر خاص بالتنقل أو بنية الصفحة | `components/layout/` |
